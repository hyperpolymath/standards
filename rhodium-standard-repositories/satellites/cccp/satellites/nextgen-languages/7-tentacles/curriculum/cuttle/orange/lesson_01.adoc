== Welcome to Juggling!

Hello, friend! I'm **Juggler Orange**! I keep things in the air!

Today, we're going to learn the basics of juggling. Don't worry - we'll start with just ONE ball!

== What You'll Learn

* How to catch and throw
* The rhythm of juggling
* Keeping track of timing

== Let's Play!

=== Activity 1: One Ball

First, let's practice with ONE ball. Easy!

[game]
--
type: single-ball
action: throw-catch
timing: slow
character: juggler-orange
--

**Juggler Orange says:** "Perfect! One ball is just: throw... catch... throw... catch!"

Now throw the ball in TIME with the beat!

[game]
--

**Juggler Orange says:** "Great timing! The secret to juggling is rhythm!"

== What You Learned (Shhh!)

You discovered:

* **Sequence:** Things happen in order (throw THEN catch)
* **Timing:** When something happens matters
* **Rhythm:** A pattern that repeats

**This is how computers do things one at a time - in sequence!**
