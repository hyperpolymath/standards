== What You Learned (Top Secret!)

You just learned:

* **Conditional logic:** IF rock THEN go around
* **Pattern matching:** Recognizing repeated sequences
* **Decision making:** Choosing the best path

**In computer science, this is called "parsing" - looking at something and deciding what to do with each piece!**

== Challenge: Create an Obstacle Course!

Design a course with:

* At least 2 rocks
* At least 1 puddle
* At least 1 boost
* A PATTERN that repeats!
