== What You Learned (Keep It Secret!)

You discovered:

* **Classification:** Grouping things that are alike
* **Categories:** Names for groups (Red things, Blue things)
* **Type checking:** Making sure something belongs

**In programming, this is called "type checking" - making sure you put the right thing in the right place!**
