== Overview

Sort items into the correct bins before time runs out! But be careful - putting the wrong thing in a bin causes an ERROR!

*Players:* 2-6
*Time:* 15-25 minutes
*Ages:* 8+ (Cuttle stage)

== Setup

1. Shuffle all Item cards
2. Deal 5 cards to each player
3. Place 3 random Bin cards in the center (shared)
4. Remaining cards form the draw pile

== How To Play

=== On Your Turn

1. **Play** 1 Item card into a matching Bin
2. **OR Draw** 1 card if you can't play
3. **Check** if any bins are full (5 items = score!)

=== Errors

If you play a card in the wrong bin:

1. Take the card back
2. Draw 1 Error card (negative points!)
3. Your turn ends

=== Wild Cards

Wild cards (ğŸŒˆ) can go in ANY bin! Use them wisely.

=== Scoring a Bin

When a bin has 5 matching items:

1. The player who placed the 5th item scores it
2. Takes all 5 cards (worth 1 point each)
3. Replace with a new Bin from the deck

== Winning

Game ends when:
* Draw pile is empty AND
* No one can play

*Scoring:*
* +1 point per item card scored
* +2 points per Wild card scored
* -2 points per Error card

Highest score wins!
