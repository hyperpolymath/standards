Amara's Story: When AI Stole Her Voice

Amara, a Nigerian diaspora poet, published "Echoes of Displacement"—a collection exploring her family's forced migration during the Biafran war. It took years to write, filled with her grandmother's memories and her own grief.

Six months later, an AI chatbot was generating cheerful travel poems using her imagery, her metaphors, her pain—stripped of context, sold as content.

She had no legal recourse. Traditional copyright didn't cover AI training.
