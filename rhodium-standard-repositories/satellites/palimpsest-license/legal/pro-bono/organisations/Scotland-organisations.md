## Scots Law Considerations

### Unique Legal Advantages
1. **Separate legal jurisdiction:** Distinct from England & Wales; continental European influence
2. **Progressive digital rights:** Scotland's Digital Strategy emphasises creator protection
3. **Strong arts policy:** National cultural strategy supports creator rights
4. **Academic excellence:** World-leading IP and technology law research

### Strategic Opportunities
1. **Test case jurisdiction:** Scottish courts as alternative to English courts for IP precedent
2. **Cultural alignment:** Strong public sector support for arts and creative industries
3. **European connections:** Scots law's civilian tradition may inform EU harmonisation
4. **Policy innovation:** Scottish Government open to innovative licensing frameworks

### Key Legal Questions for Scottish Experts
1. Scots law treatment of "moral rights" vs. economic rights
2. Interaction with UK Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1988
3. Scottish court jurisdiction for cross-border licensing disputes
4. Charitable status options for Palimpsest Stewardship Council (Scottish Charitable Incorporated Organisation - SCIO)
