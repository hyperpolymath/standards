# Palimpsest License Project Funding

This is a Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) and legal initiative, led by hyperpolymath.

## Funding Methods

The project accepts support through multiple channels:

### Recommended (for transparency)
- **Open Collective**: [palimpsest-license](https://opencollective.com/palimpsest-license)

### Other platforms
- **Patreon**: [palimpsest-license](https://patreon.com/palimpsest-license)
- **LiberaPay**: For recurring donations
- **Ko-fi**: For one-time support

### Cryptocurrency
- **Monero (XMR)**: Preferred for privacy considerations

### Corporate Sponsorship
Organizations whose values align with the license's principles are welcome to discuss corporate sponsorship. Please contact us through the repository.

## How Funds Are Used

Community backing helps maintain standards around:
- **Reliability**: Ensuring the license framework remains stable and well-documented
- **Security**: Regular security audits and updates
- **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.3 AAA compliance for all public-facing materials

## Why Support This Project?

The Palimpsest License protects creators who work with narrative, trauma, mythology, and symbolic expression. Your support helps:

1. Maintain and evolve the license framework
2. Provide legal resources and guidance
3. Build tools for compliance checking
4. Support the Stewardship Council's governance work
5. Keep documentation accessible and translated
